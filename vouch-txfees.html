Transaction Fees

Product Design

Redesigning the Checkout Experience

Company: Vouch

www.vouch.us

2023

Project background

Vouch began by serving early-stage tech startups, with an initial focus on credit card transactions. Over time, our digital platform evolved to include various payment methods, leading to increased operational costs from transaction fees.

As a Senior product designer, I was involved in this project since conception to delivery. From initial research and defining the problem to crafting the content, screens, and documentation.

I worked closely with CSR & BDR, Actuarial, User research, Product Management, legal & compliance, and engineering.

Kickoff & Initial Research

As companies sought cost-efficient solutions, we noticed an increase in price-conscious decisions, particularly regarding insurance premiums. Our user researcher and I explored how clients would respond to an additional fee in their quotes and delved into key pain points in the user journey.

Project Goals

Our project aimed to transparently transfer transaction fees to the user, ensuring clarity in their total costs. This involved intricately navigating various operational and compliance aspects unique to the insurance sector, with a focus on maintaining a seamless user experience.

There were two high level goals:

1.

Deliver a scalable solution for different payment methods and payment frequencies

2.

Clearly communicate when Clients would be receiving their certificate of insurance post-purchase

The problem statement

How did we pass transaction fees to new and renewing Clients, for Credit Cards and ACH, while remaining compliant with state and federal regulations in order to reduce operational costs and improve Client experience?
